Blocky World: Aim Trainer

Controls

Player camera controls:

  • W to move forward
  • A to move left
  • S to move back
  • D to move right
  • SHIFT to "sprint"
  • R to reset camera height
  • X to reset camera to center of world

Player mode controls:

  • 1 to enter "Mine" mining mode
  • 2 to enter "FPS" (shooting) mode
  • v to enter noclip (fly) mode

Mouse controls:

  • LEFT MOUSE BUTTON to destroy block (In MINE mode) or shoot (FPS MODE)
  • RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON to place block

Other controls:

  • ALT + 1 to switch to Range map
  • ALT + 2 to switch to Randomly generated map
  • Z to regenerate the randomly generated map
Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
